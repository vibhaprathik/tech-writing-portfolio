# Troubleshooting & FAQ – Internal Support Portal

---

## Common Issue: Users unable to login to CRM

### Steps to Resolve:
1. Check if the user exists in Active Directory.
2. Reset the user’s password and ask them to retry.
3. Ensure no recent changes in Azure AD sync.

### Known Errors:
- **401 – Unauthorized**: Usually caused by expired AD tokens  
- **User not found**: Account might be disabled or removed

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q:** Can I unlock an account manually?  
**A:** Yes, use the “Unlock User” option in the admin panel.
